Earth Tatva

6th Jan 2024

Join us for a sculpting workshop with Earth Tatva that merges art and sustainability. In this 2 hour long session, participants will delve into the world of sculpting using TatvaMix – a recyclable pottery clay crafted from up to 60% glaze-fired ceramic waste sourced from industries. Shashank Nimkar, a master sculptor, will conduct a live demonstration using the pinching technique, encouraging participants to craft 3D pieces with their hands and fingers – no tools required! The hands-on experience ensures a personal connection with the art form and the sustainable medium.
How to prepare: Empty pen refill, Toothpick, Cotton buds etc.

Anudeep’s Exclusive Workshop

7th Jan 2024

Dive into the dynamic world of product innovation with UX designer Anudeep's exclusive Workshop! Anudeep, a seasoned expert in user experience at Amazon, would be curating an engaging session focused on practical strategies for ideation, prototyping, and user-centric design. Participants can expect hands-on activities and invaluable insights to elevate their product design skills.
How to prepare: Bring along your zeal for learning new things
