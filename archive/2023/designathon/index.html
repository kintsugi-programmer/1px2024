What is a Designathon?

+

This event is to encourage and inspire the next generation of designers. Participants will work in a multidisciplinary team and learn new design methodologies and skills. They will face several challenges like the Ideation, Discovery and Develop phases.

Who is eligible to participate in the Designathon?

+

The event is open to design sIt is open to students, Enthusiasts & Professionals. Participants will use visual design concepts and understand the design process and their perspective to dive into a real-life user case.

What criteria will be used to shortlist the teams for the 1Pixel design conference?

+

Teams will be shortlisted based on their proposal and the concepts. The top 5 teams will be selected, and they will present their research and proposal at the 1Pixel design conference.

What is the theme of the Designathon?

+

The theme of the Designathon is Eco-Design: Sustainability in Action.

How are the submissions judged?

+

The registered participants will go through an initial filter process, after which five teams will get selected for the final round. The filtering process will get conducted by a judges panel. A panel of a minimum of four will witness the selected teams' final pitch. The panel will comprise industry specialists and research faculties with subject matter knowledge.

What are the requirements for submission?

+

Participants need to submit their projects as ZIP files including a research proposal and a one-page brief explaining their concepts, along with visual design concepts such as poster, prototype, presentation, video or animation (optional)

What is the timeline of the events?

+

Registrations will start on 27th December. Participants must submit a presentation and a prototype demo by 9th January. All participants will be evaluated on the service design elements discussed earlier, and the top five teams will be selected. The selected teams will be required to present their pitch on 14th January before the expert panel during the 1Pixel Design Conf. Winning teams would be evaluated based on the criteria defined above, and the winners would be awarded on 14th January during the award ceremony.
