VENUE

Our venue, Indrprastha Institute of Information Technology Delhi is conveniently located just 40 minutes from the Indira Gandhi International Airport and 60 minutes from the Delhi Railway Station. The nearest metro station, Govindpuri Metro Station, is within walking distance of the venue.

Department of Human Centered Design

The Department of Human Centered Design at IIIT-Delhi is proud to be the organiser of the 1 Pixel Design Conf'23. As an interdisciplinary undergraduate program, HCD aims to produce graduates who are well-versed in both computing and design approaches, as well as experienced in fields such as product design, user experience research, computer animation, game design, and digital media design.

Center for Design and New Media

Center for Design and New Media (CDNM), proudly supported by Tata Consultancy Services and a TCS Foundation Initiative, organizer of the 1 Pixel Design Conf'23, aims to bring together researchers in the fields of design, computer science, and new media to conduct research and education in areas such as interaction design, HCI, new media, animation, film and video, and gaming.
